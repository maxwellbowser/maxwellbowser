About Me

Hello! I am an Accelerated Master's Student at the University of Arizona, concentrated on investigating cardiac protein interactions within the Gregorio lab. I create automated data analysis techniques to enhance efficiency in my research, saving time and money. Leveraging a solid grounding in Molecular & Cellular Biology, my focus lies in comprehending the mechanics of actin-myosin interactions. I taught myself Python to improve data analysis in my lab and I am currently working on learning R and expanding my knowledge of Python. Beyond my research, I actively engage in scientific education, contributing to KXCI 91.3’s “Thesis Thursday” segment, and mentoring high school students as a coordinator in the STAR Lab.
